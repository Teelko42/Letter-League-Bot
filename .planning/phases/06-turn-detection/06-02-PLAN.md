  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
**Step 1: Start a Letter League game**
- Ensure a Letter League game is active (or about to start) in your Discord voice channel
- Confirm `DISCORD_CHANNEL_URL` is set in `.env`

**Step 2: Run calibration**
```powershell
python scripts/calibrate_turn.py
```
- Let it capture through at least:
  - 5+ frames during YOUR TURN (orange banner visible)
  - 5+ frames during OPPONENT'S TURN (no banner)
  - 2+ frames during GAME OVER (if possible — play until game ends)
- Press Ctrl+C to stop

**Step 3: Inspect results**
- Check `debug/turn_detection/` for saved screenshots
- Review the terminal output for HSV statistics per frame
- Note the HSV ranges printed for:
  - Banner-present frames (should show high orange ratio)
  - Banner-absent frames (should show low/zero orange ratio)
  - Game-over frames (should show low center peach ratio)

**Step 4: Report back with:**
- The observed HSV ranges for the orange banner (H min-max, S min-max, V min-max)
- The orange pixel ratio difference between "my turn" and "not my turn" frames
- Whether game-over screenshots were captured and their peach ratio
- The exact ROI fraction that captures the banner (is 0-15% correct, or does it need adjustment?)
- Any issues encountered (blank screenshots, wrong region, etc.)

After reporting these values, the executor will update `turn_detector.py` constants and run a verification pass against the saved screenshots.
